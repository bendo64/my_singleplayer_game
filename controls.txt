MOUNTED (Player inside ship):

MOVEMENT
W to move forward
A, D to turn
Shift to boost (needs boosters equipped)

COMBAT
Hold R to reload
Hold S to switch between ammo mode and energy mode (infinite ammo, but requires reloading)
Press Space or Left click (Introduced in 1.1) to shoot

Press E to switch between primary and secondary blaster.

1.1:
Use mouse to aim

OTHER
Press Q to dismount

DISMOUNTED (Player outside ship):

MOVEMENT
WASD to move

OTHER
Hold Space to inspect and interact
Hold Space while near ship to repair (Requires Repairtool equipped)
Press Q while near ship to mount

GENERAL :
Press X to continue dialogue
Hold Z to speed up dialogue (buggy as of V1.1)
Press C to skip dialogue WARNING : Most dialogue is to guide the player to objective, not reccomended to skip if not already seen.
Press I to expand/collapse temporary inventory UI.
