WARNING: THIS IS A FULL WALKTHROUGH OF THE PROGRESSION. SPOILERS AHEAD









BOOSTERS
Pick up the blueprint for boosters near spawn

Examine the satelite for materials

Using the fabricator, synthesize the boosters

Equip the boosters at the armory



REPAIR TOOL
Head East of spawn to find the first galaxy. Inside is a blueprint for the Repair tool. Grab it and fabricate and equip



LEVELING UP
Kill enemies at the first galaxy until around LVL 5

Go West of spawn to find the second galaxy. 

Suicide bombers will appear, they are invincible until triggerd, when they will launch at you and insta kill
Kill them before they reach you.
Farm these until around LVL 15-20


MINI BOSS
Go south-east of spawn to find the miniboss. Kill him to get the blueprint for torpedos

Fabricate and equip the torpedo weapon.


	
FINAL BOSS
Head North-West of spawn to find the Junk Ball. Blow it up using torpedos to spawn the final boss.
